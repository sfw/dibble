## What this pilot is

Dibble is an experimental AI-assisted math learning tool. Your family is
invited to use it for 6–8 weeks (Grades 4–6 math, about 20 minutes per day,
4+ days per week). It is a research pilot, not a finished product: content is
generated by an AI system with automated checks, and a human (the pilot
operator) reviews how well those checks work.

## What we collect

- **Account data:** your display name, your learner's display name and grade.
  We do not collect emails, birthdates, addresses, or photos.
- **Learning interaction data:** answers, response times, hints used,
  generated lessons and practice questions, session start/end times.
- **Derived estimates:** mastery estimates per skill, produced by the system.
- **Reports you submit:** "something's wrong with this question" reports.

## What is sent to the AI provider

Lesson and question text is generated by a third-party large-language-model
API. Requests contain curriculum text and learning-state context (e.g. "the
learner is practicing equivalent fractions and recently confused numerators
and denominators"). Requests are keyed by an internal ID, never by your
child's name. The configured provider is **[PROVIDER]** under
**[zero-retention / no-training API terms — verify and cite the exact terms
here before signing]**.

## How we use the data

Only to operate the pilot and evaluate whether Dibble teaches effectively:
weekly metrics review, content-defect triage, and a post-pilot analysis. Data
is not sold, shared, or used for advertising. Findings are reported in
aggregate or anonymized form.

## Storage and retention

Data lives in a single encrypted-at-rest server in **[REGION]** with nightly
backups retained 14 days. Pilot data is retained for up to **[N months]**
after the pilot ends for analysis, then deleted.

## Your rights

- **Export:** at any time, ask for a complete machine-readable export of your
  learner's data; we will provide it within 7 days.
- **Withdrawal:** you may withdraw at any time, no questions asked. On
  request we hard-delete all of your learner's data (including backups at the
  next rotation, within 14 days) and confirm in writing.
- **Questions:** contact **[OPERATOR NAME, EMAIL, PHONE]**.

- I understand content is AI-generated; despite automated verification, an

  button to report it.
- I understand my child should use Dibble with an adult nearby (supervised
  home use), and that it supplements — not replaces — our curriculum.
- I agree to a short weekly check-in and an exit interview.
